RAG Evaluation — LLM as Judge

Evaluate the quality of any RAG output across three RAGAS-aligned metrics using Claude as an evaluator. Paste in a question, the generated answer, and the retrieved context chunks that were used. Optionally provide a ground truth answer to also score Correctness.

Faithfulness
Is the answer grounded in the retrieved context? Penalises hallucinations — any claim not found in the chunks.
Answer Relevancy
Does the answer actually address the question? High score = on-topic and complete. Low score = off-topic or evasive.
Context Utilization
Did the retrieved chunks contain what was needed? Low score = retrieval failure — wrong chunks were fetched.
